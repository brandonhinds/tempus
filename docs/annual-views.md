# Annual Views

Yearly summaries and category breakdowns.

## Overview
- Shows yearly totals for hours and income.
- When `enable_deduction_categories` is on, includes category breakdown tables.

## Category Breakdown
- Expands a column to reveal per-deduction amounts.
- Chip-style filters focus on specific categories.

## Tips
- Use after maintaining deductions to validate annual expense spread.
- Switch year context via controls on the page.
