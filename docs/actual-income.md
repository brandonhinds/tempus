# Actual Income

Available when `enable_actual_income` is enabled.

## Purpose
- Record income actually received and compare against estimated earnings.

## Workflow
- Open Actual Income modal from Dashboard/Time pages when enabled.
- Enter gross income, super, tax, net income for a month.
- Save to update variance on the income badge and dashboard breakdowns.

## Tips
- Use after invoices are paid to keep variance meaningful.
- Check company tracking flags to ensure calculations align with your business type.
